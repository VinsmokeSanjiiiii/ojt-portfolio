Learning Insights

A reflection on my personal and professional growth, challenges faced, and key takeaways from my internship.

Overall Reflection

My On-the-Job Training experience as a Junior Programmer has been incredibly transformative. Stepping out of the academic environment and into a fast-paced, real-world development setting allowed me to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. I learned not only how to write better code, but how to write code that solves real business problems for users.

Challenges Faced

  • Adapting to a large, pre-existing legacy codebase.
  • Managing time effectively between resolving urgent support tickets and developing new features.
  • Understanding complex enterprise database structures.

Solutions & Growth

  • Leveraged version control (Git) and thorough documentation to safely navigate the codebase.
  • Improved my communication skills by collaborating daily with senior developers and supervisors.
  • Gained deeper proficiency in PostgreSQL and backend optimization.
